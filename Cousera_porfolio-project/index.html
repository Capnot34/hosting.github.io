About us

what we do

Crafting Green Oases and Nature-Inspired Experiences

Born out of a shared love for nature, Lucky Shrub is a mid-sized, family-owned garden design firm and plant nursery located in the heart of Tuscon, Arizona. We aim to create a community around the love of gardening and plant care, creating a place where both novices and seasoned gardeners can discover the joy and tranquility that comes from nurturing plants and creating beautiful outdoor spaces.

Our company was established by husband and wife team, Jason and Maria. Combining their passion for plants and entrepreneurial spirits, they embarked on a mission to make garden design and maintenance services accessible, enjoyable, and highly personalized.

As our chief garden architect, Jason brings his meticulous eye for detail and creative flair to every project. From tranquil Zen gardens to vibrant flower beds, he tailors each design to our clients' unique tastes and lifestyles. Backed by a dedicated team of skilled landscapers, Jason ensures that every garden we create is a true reflection of the client's personality and a source of pride.

On the other side of our leafy empire, Maria is the heartbeat of our brand and plant nursery. As the head of marketing and operations, she has cultivated Lucky Shrub into a trusted name within the landscaping industry. Maria's nursery is a treasure trove of indoor and outdoor plants, promising the perfect addition to your horticultural dream.

At Lucky Shrub, we're more than just a garden design firm. We're a community, a family that fosters a love for the natural world, one garden at a time.
